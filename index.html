About Mora

Mora is an AI assistant developed by AidenERM designed to help users manage information, organize tasks, understand documents, and interact with connected services through intelligent assistance.

Mora provides tools that help users work with their personal information more efficiently while keeping users in control of their data.

Features

  • AI-powered assistance and information management
  • Calendar organization and scheduling support
  • Document understanding and analysis
  • Image and file interpretation
  • Connected service integrations when authorized by the user

Google Services & Data Usage

Mora may request access to Google services only when a user explicitly grants permission through Google's authorization system.

Google Calendar access is used to provide calendar-related assistance, such as helping users understand and organize their schedules.

Gmail access, when enabled, is used only to provide email-related assistance requested by the user.

Mora does not sell user data or use Google user data for advertising. User information is handled according to the Mora Privacy Policy.

Security & Privacy

Users maintain control over their connected accounts and permissions. Mora only accesses information that users explicitly authorize.
