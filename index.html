Mora

Your personal AI assistant

Mora is an AI assistant designed to help users manage their digital life. Mora can connect with Google services to provide helpful features such as email organization, calendar assistance, reminders, and intelligent automation.

Google Data Usage

With your permission, Mora may access Google services such as Gmail and Google Calendar. This access is used only to provide requested assistant features, such as understanding emails, organizing information, and helping manage your schedule.

Features

  • AI-powered assistance
  • Gmail intelligence
  • Google Calendar integration
  • Smart reminders
  • Document and image understanding
