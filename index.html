About Mora

Mora is an AI assistant developed by AidenERM designed to help users manage their digital life through intelligent assistance.

Mora helps users organize information, understand emails, manage calendars, create reminders, and improve productivity through artificial intelligence.

Features

  • AI-powered conversations
  • Gmail intelligence
  • Google Calendar assistance
  • Smart reminders
  • Document understanding
  • Image understanding
  • Productivity assistance

Google Services Integration

Mora can connect with Google services, including Gmail and Google Calendar, when users provide permission.

Gmail access allows Mora to provide email-related features, such as organizing and understanding user emails.

Google Calendar access allows Mora to provide scheduling assistance, calendar insights, and reminder features.

Mora only uses Google data to provide requested features. User information is not sold or used for unrelated purposes.

Privacy and Security

User privacy is an important part of Mora. Users control which integrations they connect and can remove permissions at any time.

Mora follows responsible data handling practices to protect user information.
