About Mora

Mora is an artificial intelligence assistant developed by AidenERM that helps users manage their digital information, improve productivity, and interact with connected services.

Mora provides intelligent assistance through conversations, automation, and integrations designed to make managing everyday information easier.

What Mora Does

Mora provides features including:

  • AI-powered conversations and assistance
  • Gmail intelligence and email organization
  • Google Calendar assistance
  • Smart reminders and productivity tools
  • Document understanding
  • Image understanding
  • Personal information management

Google Services Integration

Mora can connect to Google services, including Gmail and Google Calendar, when users explicitly grant permission.

Gmail Access

When a user connects Gmail, Mora uses authorized Gmail data to provide requested features such as email understanding, organization, and AI-powered assistance.

Mora only accesses Gmail information required to provide features requested by the user.

Google Calendar Access

When a user connects Google Calendar, Mora uses authorized calendar data to provide calendar assistance, schedule information, reminders, and productivity features.

Data Privacy and Security

Mora respects user privacy and handles information responsibly.

  • Users choose which Google services they connect.
  • Google data is only used to provide requested Mora features.
  • Mora does not sell user information.
  • Mora does not use Google user data for advertising.
  • Users can remove permissions at any time through their Google Account settings.
